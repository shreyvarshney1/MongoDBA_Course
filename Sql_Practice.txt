#1st Question: Write an SQL query to fetch the departments that have less than five people in it. 
SELECT DEPARTMENT, COUNT(WORKER_ID) AS WorkerCount
FROM Worker
GROUP BY DEPARTMENT
HAVING COUNT(WORKER_ID) < 5;
